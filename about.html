Lan, as in "melancholy"

(My parents do not speak English so, no, it was not intentional)

My name is Lan Dao, and I live up to the "lan" in Melancholy. 

As an illustrator, or more so a storyteller, I work predominantly with black and white imagery making. Zine-making, poetry, graphic novels, and game-making are platforms to tell the stories of those who deserve to be heard.

As a concerned citizen, I choose to work with difficult emotions and social issues with a touch of dark humour. Indeed, I find (in myself, and many others) the struggle to look at the bare bone of truth without a filter.

Much of my focus has been on the oppressed "Others", the less privileged ones, the silenced ones, and the ones who struggle to get their voices across. Much to my disdain, the deeply rooted misogyny in our social construct has been quite an interest of mine.

My ethnic heritage provides me with unique insights and firsthand experience of "what is happening on the other [Eastern] side of the globe"...

I desire to use my words, my art, and the dedication of a breathing soul to sing ana anthem to commemorate them:

        those who once lived,
                   who once breathed,
                   who once fought their fight,
and those who are still struggling with it.
For sadness is just another part of joy, and grieving is just another word for loving.
